Yumcha Finance

Yumcha is a decentralised finance powered by smart contracts on the BSC network that enables the creation of pegged assets called dimSims.

Peg Mechanics

<100%

dimSims will be bought up from reserves to gradually bring the price back in line with peg (100%).

100-125%

"Bird in a cage". Market forces are allowed to operate within this boundary.

>125%

New dimSims will be minted to gradually bring the price back down in line with peg (125%).

Governance

Yumcha token holders earn profits from the minting process from the difference in price between the dimSims and the underlying asset. Yumcha tokens are paid to liquidity providers.

Yumcha dimSims are backed by the ability to exchange one dimSim for the price of the asset that it represents. For instance, a dimSim Google stock simulating 1 Google stock is exchangeable for the market value of stable coins that are held in reserves.

This is achieved via the innovative yumcha PEG mechanism. This mechanism allows yumcha users to verify the collateral on chain with an external trusted third party.
