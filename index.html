What is Yumcha Finance?

Yumcha is a decentralised finance powered by smart contracts on the BSC network that enables the creation of pegged assets called dimSims. dimSims simulate the price behaviour of real-world assets and give users access to price exposure without the burdens of owning or transacting real assets.

DimSims available include:

Vanguard Total World Stock Index Fund ( ycVT )

Coinmarketcap Total Market Capitalisation ( ycCMC )

Bloomberg Commodity Index Total Return ( ycBCOM )
